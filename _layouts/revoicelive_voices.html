Change your online voice with RevoiceLive, the easy-to-use app for macOS and Windows. Perfect for Zoom, Discord, and Google Meet, it allows you to change your voice effortlessly. Simply activate Voice Conversion in the RevoiceLive app, select RevoiceAudioDevice (on mac) or Voicemod (on win) as your microphone in your VoIP app, and start speaking.
Explore additional voices compatible with the RevoiceLive app for free!
Download your preferred voice and use the “Open” button in the app to select it.
